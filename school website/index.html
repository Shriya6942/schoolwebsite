Campus Life

school church

was established in 1978

The students come here every morning. A respected and important place in the school.



school library

was built in 1980

Students read and study quietly here. One of the more popular places as children come and hang out here during break and lunchtimes.



school auditorium

was made in 1982

Plays and shows take place here. More than 200 can be seated here and this is an amazing place for different competitions between schools to happen.
